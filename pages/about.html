Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of Ethiopian diaspora professionals, our platform began as a simple online forum for connecting Ethiopians living abroad.

What started as a small community has grown into a global platform serving tens of thousands of members across six continents. We've evolved to meet the changing needs of our community while staying true to our mission of preserving culture and creating opportunities.

Today, we're more than just a platform - we're a movement dedicated to empowering Ethiopians everywhere to connect, learn, grow, and thrive together.

Our Mission

To connect and empower Ethiopian communities worldwide through technology, education, and cultural preservation, creating opportunities for growth and development.

Our Vision

A world where every Ethiopian, regardless of location, has access to opportunities, resources, and connections needed to thrive personally and contribute to community development.
